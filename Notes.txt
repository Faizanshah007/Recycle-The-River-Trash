Quantifying ecological alteration
of rivers is complex as various flow components need to be
considered due to their differing impacts on riverine ecosystems.
Therefore, total ecochange is not necessarily a good indicator of
ecological alteration, as has been confirmed by studies (Gao et al.,
2009).
